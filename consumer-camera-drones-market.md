Fixed Wing Systems resemble traditional airplanes and offer longer flight times and greater distances, making them ideal for surveying large areas. Multirotor Systems, like quadcopters, are popular for their stability and maneuverability, suited for aerial photography and recreational use. Other Systems encompass unique configurations, such as hybrid drones, which combine elements of both fixed wing and multirotor designs for specialized applications.</p></p>

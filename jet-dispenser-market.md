<p><strong>The Jet Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Jetting</li><li>Non-Contact Jetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jet Dispenser market is segmented into two primary types: Contact Jetting and Non-Contact Jetting. Contact Jetting involves physical contact between the dispensing nozzle and the material, ensuring precise application for high-viscosity liquids and specific patterns. In contrast, Non-Contact Jetting employs methods that create droplets without nozzle contact, enabling faster operations and reducing contamination risks. This segmentation caters to diverse industries, allowing for tailored dispensing solutions that meet varying precision and speed requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Toroidal alloy powder cores are used in various electronic applications due to their magnetic properties. MPP (Molybdenum Permalloy Powder) cores offer high permeability and low core losses, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Sendust powder cores, composed of iron, silicon, and aluminum, provide excellent saturation levels. High Flux powder cores are optimized for high inductance and low losses. Fe-Si (Iron-Silicon) powder cores, known for their cost-effectiveness, are useful in standard applications. Other types may include custom alloys for specialized functions.</p></p>

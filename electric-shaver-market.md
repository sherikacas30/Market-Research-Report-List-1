<p><ul><li>Foil</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric shaver market primarily features two types: foil and rotary shavers. Foil shavers use oscillating blades behind a thin layer of foil, offering a close shave ideal for straight, fine hair. They work best on flat surfaces and are favored for precision. In contrast, rotary shavers utilize circular, spinning blades that adapt to the contours of the face, making them suitable for thicker hair and various shaving angles. Both types cater to different user preferences and shaving needs.</p></p>

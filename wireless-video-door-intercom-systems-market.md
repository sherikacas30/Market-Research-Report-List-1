<p><p>Wireless Video Door Intercom Systems are categorized primarily into Wi-Fi and Radio Frequency (RF) types. Wi-Fi systems leverage existing internet connections, allowing for real-time video streaming and remote access via smartphones or tablets. They offer high-definition video quality and versatile connectivity but depend on stable Wi-Fi. In contrast, RF systems utilize dedicated radio frequencies for communication, providing reliable connections without the need for internet. While typically easier to install, RF systems may have limitations in range and video quality compared to Wi-Fi solutions.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Flame Resistant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame-resistant hydraulic fluid market comprises four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-based and contain a significant water content, enhancing safety against fire. HFB fluids are also water-containing but are less than HFA in terms of water percentage. HFC fluids are water-containing but with a higher proportion of synthetic fluids, offering superior protection in high-temperature environments. Lastly, HFD fluids are non-water-containing synthetic fluids, designed for extreme conditions, providing excellent thermal stability and fire resistance.</p></p>

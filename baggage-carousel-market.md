<p><ul><li>Single-Level</li><li>Multi-Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baggage carousels can be categorized into single-level and multi-level markets based on their design and operational efficiency. Single-level carousels feature a single circular belt at one height, simplifying passenger access but limiting space. In contrast, multi-level carousels consist of stacked belts, maximizing space and accommodating more baggage in crowded airports. This dual approach allows airports to optimize baggage claim areas, catering to varying passenger volumes and improving overall efficiency in baggage handling systems.</p></p>

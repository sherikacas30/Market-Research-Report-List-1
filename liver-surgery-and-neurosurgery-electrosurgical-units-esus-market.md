The market is divided into three main types: monopole electrosurgical units, which employ a single active electrode for cutting and coagulation; bipolar electrosurgical units that utilize two electrodes to control current and minimize collateral damage; and vessel sealing units designed to coagulate and seal blood vessels effectively, reducing bleeding during surgery. These technologies improve surgical outcomes and patient recovery times in complex procedures.</p></p>

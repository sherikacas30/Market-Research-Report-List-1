<p><p>The Tellurium Dioxide (TeO2) market comprises two primary types: α-TeO2 and β-TeO2. α-TeO2, or alpha tellurium dioxide, is a stable, tetragonal crystalline form widely used in optical applications, such as acousto-optic devices, due to its high refractive index and transparency. β-TeO2, or beta tellurium dioxide, is a less common orthorhombic form, often utilized in specialized electronic and catalytic applications. Both forms are essential in industries like electronics, optics, and energy, with demand driven by their unique structural and functional properties.</p></p>

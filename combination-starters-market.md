<p><strong>The Combination Starters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Voltage Non Reversing</li><li>Full-Voltage Reversing</li><li>Multi-Speed</li><li>Reduced Voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Combination starters are devices used to control electric motors by combining protection and switching functions. </p><p>- **Full-Voltage Non-Reversing** starters provide direct-on-line operation, offering simplicity for applications that do not require directional change. </p><p>- **Full-Voltage Reversing** starters allow for forward and reverse motor operation, suitable for applications needing bidirectional control. </p><p>- **Multi-Speed** starters enable varied motor speeds, benefiting processes requiring adjustments in speed. </p><p>- **Reduced Voltage** starters limit initial voltage to reduce inrush current, protecting both the motor and connected equipment during startup.</p></p>

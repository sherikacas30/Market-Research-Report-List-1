<p><ul><li>LOX</li><li>OBOGS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The onboard oxygen systems market is categorized into three main types: Liquid Oxygen (LOX), On-Board Oxygen Generation Systems (OBOGS), and others. LOX systems store oxygen in liquid form, providing a compact and efficient solution for oxygen supply, particularly in aviation. OBOGS generates oxygen from ambient air using molecular sieves, offering continuous supply without the need for onboard storage. Other systems may include portable oxygen systems and emergency oxygen kits, catering to various applications across military and commercial aviation sectors.</p></p>

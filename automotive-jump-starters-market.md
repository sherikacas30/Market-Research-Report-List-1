<p><p>Automotive jump starters are portable devices used to start vehicles with dead batteries. They come in two main types based on battery technology: Lithium-Ion and Lead-Acid. Lithium-Ion jump starters are lightweight, compact, and offer high energy density, making them ideal for modern vehicles and easy to carry. Lead-Acid jump starters are bulkier, heavier, and more affordable, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types provide reliable power, but Lithium-Ion models are preferred for convenience, while Lead-Acid remains a cost-effective choice for larger vehicles.</p></p>

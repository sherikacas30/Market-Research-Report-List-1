<p><ul><li>Drum Brake</li><li>Caliper Disc Brake</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crane brake market is primarily categorized into three types: drum brakes, caliper disc brakes, and others. Drum brakes consist of a cylindrical drum and friction shoes, offering reliable stopping power for heavy loads. Caliper disc brakes utilize a disc and hydraulic calipers for superior heat dissipation and braking efficiency, making them ideal for high-performance cranes. The "others" category encompasses various braking systems, including magnetic and regenerative brakes, catering to specialized requirements and applications in the crane industry.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Series Reactor</li><li>Shunt Reactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Voltage Reactor market encompasses two main types: series reactors and shunt reactors. Series reactors are utilized to limit fault currents and stabilize voltage levels in transmission lines, enhancing system reliability and efficiency. Conversely, shunt reactors are primarily employed to manage reactive power and mitigate voltage fluctuations in long transmission networks. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the stability and performance of electrical systems while ensuring the safe transmission of high voltage electricity.</p></p>

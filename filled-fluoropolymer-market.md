<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>PFA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filled fluoropolymer market primarily includes PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy). PTFE is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties, often used in applications like seals, gaskets, and non-stick coatings. PFA, while similar in chemical resistance, offers better thermal stability and processability, making it ideal for tubing and high-temperature applications. Both types can be enhanced with fillers like glass or carbon to improve mechanical properties, making them suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive spark coils market is categorized into single-spark and multi-spark systems. Single-spark coils provide one spark per engine cycle, efficiently igniting the air-fuel mixture in traditional combustion engines. In contrast, multi-spark systems deliver multiple sparks, enhancing combustion efficiency, reducing emissions, and improving power output, particularly in high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific engine designs and performance needs, influencing the market based on factors like fuel efficiency, emissions standards, and the demand for enhanced engine performance.</p></p>

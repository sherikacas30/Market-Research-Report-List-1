<p><p>The oral disintegrating and dissolving film market encompasses products designed for quick dissolution in the mouth. Orodispersible films, which quickly dissolve upon contact with saliva, are often used for delivering medication without the need for water, benefiting patients with swallowing difficulties. Oromucosal films, on the other hand, adhere to the mucosal lining of the mouth and deliver active ingredients systemically. Both types offer improved patient compliance and enhanced bioavailability, addressing the needs of various therapeutic areas.</p></p>

<p><p>The SSL certification market consists of three primary types: Domain Validation (DV), Organization Validation (OV), and Extended Validation (EV) SSL certificates. DV certificates confirm domain ownership and are quick to obtain, ideal for personal websites. OV certificates verify the organization’s identity, providing more trust for business sites. EV certificates offer the highest level of security, requiring extensive verification of the organization before issuance, and displaying the organization's name in the browser, boosting customer confidence significantly.</p></p>

<p><p>The Food Grade Fumed Silica market is categorized based on surface area, indicated by BET (Brunauer-Emmett-Teller) values. Types include BET 100-160, BET 160-210, and BET 210-300. BET 100-160 offers moderate surface area, ideal for thickening agents, while BET 160-210 provides higher surface area for improved flow and anti-caking properties. BET 210-300 features a high surface area, enhancing viscosity and stability in formulations. "Others" encompasses specialty grades tailored for specific applications in food and beverage industries.</p></p>

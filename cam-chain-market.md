<p><p>The cam chain market includes roller chains and silent chains, both used for power transmission in engines and machinery. Roller chains are durable, cost-effective, and ideal for high-speed applications, commonly found in motorcycles and industrial equipment. Silent chains, also known as inverted tooth chains, operate quietly and smoothly, making them suitable for precision-driven systems like automotive engines. Both types ensure synchronized timing and efficient power transfer, with roller chains excelling in rugged environments and silent chains in noise-sensitive applications.</p></p>

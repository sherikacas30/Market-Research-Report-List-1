<p><p>The resin dental material market is categorized into several types based on filler particle sizes. Macrofilled fillers offer durability and strength, making them suitable for posterior restorations. Microfilled fillers provide excellent aesthetics and polishability, ideal for anterior applications. Hybrid fillers combine the benefits of both, balancing strength and aesthetics. Nanofilled fillers offer superior polishability and reduced wear, suitable for demanding aesthetic applications. Bulk fillers are designed for quick placement in large cavities, emphasizing efficiency without compromising performance.</p></p>

<p><p>The Free Triiodothyronine Test Kit market is categorized into two primary types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay uses light-emitting reactions to detect free triiodothyronine levels in serum, ensuring high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, Time-resolved Immunoassay employs time-dependent measurements of fluorescence, enhancing detection accuracy while minimizing background interference. Both methods facilitate accurate diagnosis of thyroid-related disorders, contributing to effective patient management and treatment strategies in endocrinology.</p></p>

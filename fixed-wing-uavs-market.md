<p><p>Fixed-wing UAVs are categorized into two primary market types based on their power sources: power-driven UAVs and fuel-driven UAVs. Power-driven UAVs utilize electric motors powered by batteries, offering quieter operation and reduced emissions, making them suitable for urban environments and smaller missions. In contrast, fuel-driven UAVs rely on internal combustion engines that utilize liquid fuels, providing longer flight times and greater payload capacities, making them ideal for extensive surveillance and agricultural applications. Both types cater to diverse industry needs and operational requirements.</p></p>

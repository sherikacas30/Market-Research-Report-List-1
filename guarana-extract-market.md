<p><p>Guarana extract is available in two main types: dry extract and fluid extract. Dry extract is created by removing moisture from guarana seeds, resulting in a concentrated powder that is often used in supplements and energy products. Fluid extract, on the other hand, involves extracting the active compounds using solvent processes, yielding a liquid form that is easily incorporated into beverages and tinctures. Both types are valued for their stimulating effects and are utilized in various health and wellness applications.</p></p>

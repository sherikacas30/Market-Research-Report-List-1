<p><p>Air purification systems can be categorized into several types. HEPA purifiers use high-efficiency particulate air filters to capture small particles, ensuring clean air. Activated carbon purifiers eliminate odors and harmful gases by adsorbing them. Static electricity purifiers use electrical charges to attract and trap pollutants. Ion and ozone generator purifiers release ions or ozone molecules to neutralize contaminants. Other market types may include ultraviolet light purifiers and photochemical oxidants, each targeting specific pollutants for improving indoor air quality.</p></p>

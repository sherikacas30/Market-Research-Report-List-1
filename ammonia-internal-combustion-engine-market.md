<p><strong>The Ammonia Internal Combustion Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spark-Ignition Engine</li><li>Compression-Ignition Engine</li><li>Gas-Turbine Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ammonia internal combustion engine market features three main types: spark-ignition engines, compression-ignition engines, and gas-turbine engines. Spark-ignition engines ignite ammonia fuel using an electric spark, suitable for lighter applications. Compression-ignition engines rely on high pressure to ignite the ammonia, making them more efficient for heavy-duty uses. Gas-turbine engines utilize ammonia in a continuous flow, ideal for large-scale power generation. Each type leverages ammonia's potential as a clean energy source, reducing carbon emissions in various applications.</p></p>

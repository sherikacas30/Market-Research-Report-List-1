<p><strong>The Industrial Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li><li>Momentum Transfer Pumps</li><li>Entrapment Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum pump market comprises three main types: Positive Displacement Pumps, which operate by trapping a fixed volume of gas and forcing it out, thus creating a vacuum; Momentum Transfer Pumps, which utilize high-speed rotating blades to transfer momentum to gas molecules, effectively removing them; and Entrapment Pumps, which capture gas molecules through physical or chemical means, creating a vacuum. Each type serves specific applications within various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and manufacturing.</p></p>

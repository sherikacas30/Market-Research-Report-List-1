<p><strong>The Steel Refractory Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel refractory material market is primarily divided into two types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories consist of clay and various additives, offering durability and thermal resistance, making them suitable for lining furnaces and kilns. Monolithic refractories, on the other hand, are castable materials that allow for seamless applications and can be shaped on-site, providing flexibility in installation. Both types are essential for maintaining the structural integrity of steel production processes under extreme temperatures.</p></p>

Two-Component Polyurethane requires mixing a resin with a hardener, offering enhanced durability and performance ideal for demanding environments. Urethane-modified products combine properties of both polyurethane and other materials, resulting in improved flexibility, adhesion, and chemical resistance, catering to specialized industrial needs.</p></p>

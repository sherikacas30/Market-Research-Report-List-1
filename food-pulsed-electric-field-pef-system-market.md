<p><p>The Food Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) System market includes several key components. The **Pulse Generator** creates high-voltage pulses essential for the PEF process. The **Control Switch** regulates the electric field, ensuring precise operation. The **Feed Pump** moves the food product through the system, maintaining flow and consistency. The **Processing System** encompasses the main setup for treating food, improving extraction and preservation. Lastly, the **Cooling System** manages heat produced during processing, ensuring product quality and safety.</p></p>

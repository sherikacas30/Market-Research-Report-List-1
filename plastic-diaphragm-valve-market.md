<p><p>The plastic diaphragm valve market is categorized into electric and manual types. Electric diaphragm valves are operated using electric actuators, providing precision control and automation, ideal for processes requiring consistent flow regulation. In contrast, manual diaphragm valves are operated by hand, allowing for straightforward on-off control without the need for external power. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries like water treatment, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals, emphasizing durability and resistance to corrosive substances inherent in plastic materials.</p></p>

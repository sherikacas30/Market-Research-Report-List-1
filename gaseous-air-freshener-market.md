<p><strong>The Gaseous Air Freshener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ozone</li><li>Negative Ion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gaseous air freshener market includes two notable types: ozone generators and negative ion generators. Ozone generators emit ozone to neutralize odors and purify the air, targeting bacteria and pollutants effectively. Conversely, negative ion generators release negative ions that bind with positively charged particles, such as dust and allergens, causing them to settle, thus improving air quality. Both technologies cater to health-conscious consumers seeking effective, chemical-free solutions for maintaining a fresh and pleasant indoor environment.</p></p>

<p><p>The industrial motors market is primarily divided into high voltage and low voltage motors. High voltage motors operate at voltages above 1 kV and are commonly used in large-scale applications such as manufacturing, mining, and oil and gas due to their efficiency and robustness. In contrast, low voltage motors function at voltages below 1 kV, serving smaller applications like pumps, fans, and conveyors. Both types play crucial roles in various industries by driving machinery, enhancing productivity, and ensuring operational reliability.</p></p>

<p><p>The silk thread trap market is primarily segmented into two types: the Goose-neck trap and the En Trap. The Goose-neck trap features a curved design that effectively captures silk threads while minimizing damage, making it popular among silk producers. In contrast, the En Trap utilizes a specialized mesh that enhances visibility and retention of silk threads, facilitating easier collection. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to different operational needs within the silk production industry, ensuring efficient harvesting and quality retention.</p></p>

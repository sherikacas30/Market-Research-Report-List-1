<p><p>The Interventional Cardiology Catheter market comprises three main types: Open-Heart Surgery, Off-Pump Heart Surgery, and Minimally Invasive Heart Surgery. Open-Heart Surgery involves procedures requiring chest incision, allowing direct access to the heart. Off-Pump Heart Surgery performs coronary artery bypass without stopping the heart, utilizing specialized stabilizers. Minimally Invasive Heart Surgery employs smaller incisions and advanced techniques, reducing recovery time and complications. Each type addresses specific cardiovascular conditions while reflecting advancements in surgical technology and patient care.</p></p>

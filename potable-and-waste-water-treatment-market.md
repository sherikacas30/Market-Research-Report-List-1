<p><p>The potable and wastewater treatment market encompasses two main types of treatment methods: chemical and physical device treatments. Chemical treatment involves the use of chemical processes, such as coagulation and disinfection, to remove contaminants and purify water for safe consumption. In contrast, physical device treatment utilizes mechanical devices, such as filtration and sedimentation, to separate impurities and solid particles from water. Both methods are essential for ensuring clean drinking water and effective wastewater management, addressing various contamination challenges.</p></p>

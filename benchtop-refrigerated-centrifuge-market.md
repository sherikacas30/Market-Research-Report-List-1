<p><ul><li>High Speed Benchtop Refrigerated Centrifuges</li><li>Low Speed Benchtop Refrigerated Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop refrigerated centrifuges are classified into two main types: high-speed and low-speed centrifuges. High-speed benchtop refrigerated centrifuges are designed for rapid separation of samples at high rotational speeds, making them ideal for applications in molecular biology and clinical laboratories. In contrast, low-speed benchtop refrigerated centrifuges operate at lower speeds, suitable for separating larger particles and samples, often used in cell culture and sedimentation tasks. Both types maintain low temperatures to protect sensitive samples during centrifugation.</p></p>

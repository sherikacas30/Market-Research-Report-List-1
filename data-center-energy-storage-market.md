<p><p>The data center energy storage market encompasses various technologies aimed at enhancing energy efficiency and reliability. Batteries, including lithium-ion and flow batteries, provide energy for short- to medium-term use. Supercapacitors offer rapid charge and discharge capabilities for quick bursts of energy. Flywheels store kinetic energy for short-duration applications, improving system stability. Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES) utilizes air compression to store energy for longer durations. Other technologies may include thermal storage and innovative chemical solutions, expanding the landscape of energy management in data centers.</p></p>

<p><p>The steel tracked pads market is categorized into three main types based on steel composition: high manganese steel, medium manganese steel, and low-alloy steel. High manganese steel offers outstanding wear resistance and toughness, making it ideal for high-impact applications. Medium manganese steel balances durability and cost-effectiveness, suitable for moderate wear conditions. Low-alloy steel provides a more affordable option with decent strength and toughness, often used in less demanding environments. Each type caters to specific operational needs in heavy machinery and construction.</p></p>

<p><p>The Automotive Heater Control Valves market includes two primary types: Electric and Hydraulic. Electric valves use electronic signals to regulate coolant flow, offering precise temperature control and integration with modern vehicle systems. Hydraulic valves rely on mechanical or fluid pressure to operate, often found in older or simpler vehicle designs. Both types manage cabin heating by controlling the flow of hot coolant through the heater core, ensuring optimal comfort and efficiency in automotive climate control systems.</p></p>

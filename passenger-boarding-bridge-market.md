<p><p>A Passenger Boarding Bridge (PBB), also known as a jet bridge or aerobridge, is an enclosed, movable connector that extends from an airport terminal gate to an aircraft, allowing passengers to board and disembark safely and efficiently. It enhances passenger convenience, improves operational efficiency, and ensures compliance with safety and security standards. PBBs are widely used in commercial airports and are available in various types, including apron drive, nose-loader, and dual bridges, catering to different aircraft models and airport requirements.</p><p>The Passenger Boarding Bridge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing air passenger traffic, expansion and modernization of airport infrastructure, and the rising demand for advanced boarding solutions. Emerging economies are investing heavily in airport development, further propelling market growth. Additionally, the integration of smart technologies, such as automated and energy-efficient PBBs, is a key trend shaping the market. These innovations aim to reduce operational costs and enhance passenger experience. The focus on sustainability and the adoption of eco-friendly materials in PBB manufacturing are also gaining traction. Overall, the market is poised for steady expansion, supported by global aviation industry growth and technological advancements.</p></p>

Steel-Walled PBBs are constructed with durable metal walls, providing enhanced strength and security, commonly used in high-traffic or harsh weather conditions.

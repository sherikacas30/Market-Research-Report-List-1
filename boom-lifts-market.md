<p><p>The boom lifts market is categorized into several types, each serving specific applications. Telescopic boom lifts extend vertically, offering high reach and platform capacity. Articulating boom lifts provide flexibility with their jointed arms, enabling access to tight spaces. Trailer-mounted boom lifts are portable and can be towed by vehicles, ideal for short-term jobs. Bucket trucks combine a truck chassis with an elevated platform, commonly used for utility work. Towable boom lifts offer similar portability, suitable for various outdoor tasks.</p></p>

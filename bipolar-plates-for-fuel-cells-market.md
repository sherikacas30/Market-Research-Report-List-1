Graphite plates offer excellent conductivity and corrosion resistance but can be brittle. Metal plates, such as stainless steel, provide high strength and durability, although they may have issues with corrosion. Composite plates combine materials for enhanced performance, balancing weight, cost, and conductivity while addressing some limitations of purely graphite or metal options.</p></p>

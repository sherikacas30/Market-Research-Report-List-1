<p><p>The Endoscopic Ultrasound Guided Needle market comprises two primary types: aspiration needles and biopsy needles. Aspiration needles are designed to draw fluid samples from cystic lesions or abscesses, allowing for diagnostic analysis. In contrast, biopsy needles are utilized to obtain tissue samples from solid masses, facilitating the assessment of tumors or other abnormalities. Both needle types enhance diagnostic accuracy, play a crucial role in minimally invasive procedures, and cater to various clinical needs in endoscopic ultrasound applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Acoustic-Insulation Rock Wool Market offers various types tailored for soundproofing and insulation needs. Strip rock wool is designed for installation between studs and joists, providing targeted noise control. Tubular rock wool, with its cylindrical shape, fits around pipes and ducts, minimizing noise transmission in HVAC systems. Granular rock wool offers flexible application, suitable for filling gaps and voids, enhancing overall sound insulation.</p></p>

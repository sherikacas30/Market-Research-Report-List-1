<p><p>The pin gauges market is primarily divided into two types: straight type and shank type. Straight type pin gauges are solid cylindrical tools used for measuring diameters and tolerances in various engineering applications, providing high accuracy. Shank type pin gauges, on the other hand, feature a tapered end that can be inserted into holes or slots to check dimensions and alignments. Both types serve crucial roles in quality control and precision measurement across industries such as manufacturing and automotive.</p></p>

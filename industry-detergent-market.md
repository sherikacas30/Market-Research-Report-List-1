<p><strong>The Industry Detergent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surfactant</li><li>Acid</li><li>Base</li><li>Strong Oxidants</li><li>Enzyme</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial detergent market consists of various types, including surfactants, acids, bases, strong oxidants, enzymes, and others. Surfactants lower surface tension, enhancing cleaning efficiency. Acids, like phosphoric acid, are used for rust removal and mineral deposit cleaning. Bases, such as sodium hydroxide, neutralize acidity and break down organic materials. Strong oxidants, such as hydrogen peroxide, provide powerful stain removal. Enzymes target specific stains by breaking down proteins, fats, and carbohydrates, while 'others' encompass specialty formulations catering to unique cleaning needs. </p></p>

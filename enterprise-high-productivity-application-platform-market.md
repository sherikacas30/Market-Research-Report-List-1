Managed platforms involve third-party vendors maintaining the infrastructure, enabling organizations to focus on development while benefiting from vendor expertise. Hybrid models combine both approaches, allowing businesses to leverage on-premises capabilities while utilizing cloud resources for scalability and flexibility, enhancing operational efficiency and responsiveness to changing demands.</p></p>

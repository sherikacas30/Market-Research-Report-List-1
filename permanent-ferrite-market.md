<p><ul><li>Sintered Permanent Ferrite</li><li>Bonded Permanent Ferrite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permanent ferrite market comprises two primary types: sintered permanent ferrite and bonded permanent ferrite. Sintered permanent ferrite, processed by high-temperature sintering, offers superior magnetic properties and is widely used in motors, speakers, and sensors. In contrast, bonded permanent ferrite involves combining ferrite materials with a polymer binder, resulting in flexibility and ease of shaping. This type is popular in applications requiring lightweight and complex geometries, such as in miniaturized electronic devices and automotive components.</p></p>

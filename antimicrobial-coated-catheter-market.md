<p><p>The antimicrobial coated catheter market includes several key types of coatings. Antibiotic coatings involve embedding antibiotics within the catheter material to prevent bacterial adhesion and infection. Silver ion coatings leverage the antimicrobial properties of silver to inhibit bacterial growth, enhancing the catheter's safety. Protein nano bacteriostatic coatings utilize proteins engineered at the nanoscale to create a barrier that discourages bacterial colonization while promoting biocompatibility. Together, these innovations aim to reduce catheter-associated infections and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>

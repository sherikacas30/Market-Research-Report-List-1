<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast iron gate valves market is segmented into two main types based on connection methods: flanged and threaded connections. Flanged connection valves feature a flat, protruding flange that allows for easy attachment to piping systems using bolts, ensuring a secure and leak-proof seal. Threaded connection valves instead use internal or external threads to facilitate direct attachment to pipes, offering flexibility in installation. Both types are essential for controlling fluid flow in various industrial applications, each catering to specific installation and operational needs.</p></p>

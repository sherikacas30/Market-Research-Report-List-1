<p><p>Digital Light Processing (DLP) projectors are categorized mainly into 1-chip and 3-chip models. </p><p>1-chip DLP projectors utilize a single DLP chip and a color wheel, making them affordable and compact, suitable for home theater and small venue use. </p><p>Conversely, 3-chip DLP projectors incorporate three separate chips for red, green, and blue, offering superior color accuracy and brightness, ideal for professional settings like large auditoriums and high-end presentations. Both types cater to varied consumer and business needs.</p></p>

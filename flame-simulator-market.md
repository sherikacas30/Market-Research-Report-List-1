The UV market utilizes ultraviolet sensors to detect flames through unique radiation patterns, ideal for quick response. The IR market employs infrared sensors, which are effective in diverse environmental conditions and can detect flames from a distance. The UVIR market combines both technologies, enhancing detection reliability and accuracy by leveraging the strengths of each type, making it suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>

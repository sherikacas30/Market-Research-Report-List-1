<p><p>The de-inked pulp market encompasses various types based on the pulping process. Mechanical pulping involves grinding wood chips to separate fibers, resulting in lower quality pulp, often used in newsprint and tissue production. Chemical pulping employs chemicals to dissolve lignin, yielding higher-quality pulp suitable for premium paper products. Other methods may include semi-chemical or recycled pulp processes that blend techniques for specific applications. Each type caters to diverse paper manufacturing needs, balancing quality and cost considerations.</p></p>

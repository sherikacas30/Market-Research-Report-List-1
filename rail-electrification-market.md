<p><strong>The Rail Electrification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC System</li><li>AC System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail electrification involves using electrical systems to power trains, primarily categorized into DC and AC systems. The DC system typically operates at lower voltages (750V or 1,500V) and is commonly used in urban transit networks due to its efficiency in short-distance travel. In contrast, the AC system operates at higher voltages (usually 15kV or 25kV), making it suitable for long-distance and high-speed rail applications. Both systems are crucial for reducing emissions and enhancing overall rail transport efficiency.</p></p>

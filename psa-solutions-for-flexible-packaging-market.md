<p><p>The PSA solutions for flexible packaging encompass three main adhesive types: solvent-based adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities but rely on volatile organic compounds, making them less environmentally friendly. Solvent-free adhesives, on the other hand, are more sustainable, ensuring minimal environmental impact while providing robust adhesion. Water-based adhesives are safe and environmentally friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them ideal for eco-conscious applications. Each type caters to specific packaging needs and performance requirements.</p></p>

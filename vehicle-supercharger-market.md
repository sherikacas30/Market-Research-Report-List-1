<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vehicle Supercharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots Supercharger</li><li>Twin-Screw Supercharger</li><li>Centrifugal Supercharger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle supercharger market consists of three main types: Roots superchargers, twin-screw superchargers, and centrifugal superchargers. Roots superchargers provide immediate boost and are known for their simplicity and reliability, making them ideal for low-end torque. Twin-screw superchargers offer higher efficiency and compact design, delivering consistent power throughout the RPM range. Centrifugal superchargers use a rotating impeller to compress air, providing a power band that increases with engine speed, making them popular for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct performance needs.</p></p>

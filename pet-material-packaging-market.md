<p><p>The PET material packaging market comprises two primary types: rigid and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging includes bottles, containers, and jars that maintain their shape and are ideal for products like beverages and bulk goods. In contrast, flexible packaging consists of bags, pouches, and wraps that can easily conform to the contents and are often used for snacks and perishables. Both types offer benefits such as durability, protection, and recyclability, catering to diverse product needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>

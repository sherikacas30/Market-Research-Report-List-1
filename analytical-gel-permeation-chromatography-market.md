<p><p>The Analytical Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC) market is segmented into ambient temperature and high-temperature types. Ambient temperature GPC operates at lower temperatures, ideal for sensitive polymers and biological samples, ensuring minimal degradation during analysis. In contrast, high-temperature GPC is utilized for analyzing high-performance polymers, allowing for better solubility and resolution by increasing molecular mobility. Each type caters to specific applications, with ambient temperature focusing on stability and high-temperature emphasizing enhanced analytical capabilities for complex materials.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Refractory Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory market is divided into shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are pre-formed, heat-resistant materials like bricks and tiles, used in high-temperature applications such as furnaces and kilns. They offer structural stability and durability. Unshaped refractories, including castables, mortars, and gunning mixes, are monolithic materials applied in situ, providing flexibility and seamless linings. Both types are essential in industries like steel, cement, and glass, ensuring thermal insulation, corrosion resistance, and prolonged equipment life under extreme conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The nano titanium dioxide powder market is primarily divided into two types: rutile and anatase. Rutile type features a high refractive index and superior UV protection, making it ideal for coatings, plastics, and cosmetics. Conversely, anatase type offers better photocatalytic properties, often utilized in environmental applications such as self-cleaning surfaces and air purification. Both types cater to distinct applications and industries, reflecting their unique properties and benefits in enhancing performance and sustainability in various products.</p></p>

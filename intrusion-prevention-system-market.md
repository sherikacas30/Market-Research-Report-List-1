<p><strong>The Intrusion Prevention System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Network-Based Intrusion Prevention System (NIPS)</li><li>Wireless Intrusion Prevention Systems (WIPS)</li><li>Network Behavior Analysis (NBA)</li><li>Host-Based Intrusion Prevention System (HIPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intrusion Prevention System (IPS) market encompasses several types, each tailored for specific use cases. Network-Based Intrusion Prevention Systems (NIPS) monitor network traffic to detect and prevent malicious activities at the perimeter. Wireless Intrusion Prevention Systems (WIPS) protect wireless networks by identifying and mitigating intrusion attempts. Network Behavior Analysis (NBA) focuses on identifying anomalies in network traffic patterns. Host-Based Intrusion Prevention Systems (HIPS) operate on individual devices, monitoring system activities to thwart intrusions at the host level.</p></p>

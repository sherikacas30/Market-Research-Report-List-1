<p><p>The Virtual Dental Consultation Service market can be categorized into two main types: synchronous and asynchronous consultation services. Synchronous consultation services involve real-time interactions between patients and dental professionals, facilitating immediate feedback and discussion through video calls or live chats. In contrast, asynchronous consultation services allow patients to send inquiries or share dental concerns at their convenience, with dentists responding later. This flexibility caters to diverse patient needs and preferences, enhancing accessibility and efficiency in dental care delivery.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD camera market is primarily divided into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture images in a single frame, ideal for static scenes and applications like industrial inspection and medical imaging. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suited for high-speed moving objects and continuous processes, such as conveyor belt quality control or scanning large surfaces. Each type serves distinct needs based on application requirements and imaging technology.</p></p>

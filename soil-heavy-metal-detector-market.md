<p><p>A soil heavy metal detector is a device used to measure and analyze the concentration of heavy metals such as lead, cadmium, arsenic, mercury, and chromium in soil. These detectors are essential for environmental monitoring, agricultural safety, and industrial compliance, as heavy metal contamination can pose significant risks to human health, ecosystems, and food security. They utilize advanced technologies like X-ray fluorescence (XRF), atomic absorption spectroscopy (AAS), and inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry (ICP-MS) to provide accurate and rapid results.</p><p>The Soil Heavy Metal Detector Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of environmental pollution, stricter government regulations, and the need for sustainable agricultural practices.

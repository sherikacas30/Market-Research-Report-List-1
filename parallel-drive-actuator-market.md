<p><p>The Parallel Drive Actuator market is categorized into two main types based on the gear configurations: those employing spur gears and those utilizing spiral gears. Spur gear actuators offer direct, efficient motion transfer and are known for their simplicity and high precision, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, spiral gear actuators provide smoother operation and reduced noise due to their curved teeth design, making them ideal for applications requiring higher torque and minimal backlash, often found in industrial settings.</p></p>

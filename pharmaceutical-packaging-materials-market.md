<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical packaging materials encompass various adhesive types essential for ensuring product integrity and safety. Solvent adhesives contain organic solvents that provide strong bonding but may pose environmental and health risks. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate harmful solvents, making them safer for both users and the environment while maintaining effective adhesion. Waterborne adhesives use water as a solvent, offering a non-toxic alternative that is environmentally friendly and suitable for sensitive applications. Each type plays a crucial role in packaging pharmaceuticals efficiently and sustainably.</p></p>

<p><p>The specialty label adhesive market comprises primarily two types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly properties and easy cleanup, making them suitable for various applications like food packaging. Conversely, solvent-based label adhesives use organic solvents, providing strong bonding, quick-drying capabilities, and resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for industrial uses. Both types cater to specific needs, influencing their applications across diverse industries.</p></p>

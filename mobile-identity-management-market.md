<p><p>The Mobile Identity Management (MIM) market is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-based solutions. On-Premise MIM involves managing mobile identities through local servers and infrastructure, providing organizations with enhanced control and security over their data. In contrast, Cloud-based MIM utilizes remote servers managed by third-party providers, offering scalability and flexibility, as well as reduced maintenance overhead. Both types aim to secure mobile devices, applications, and data while enabling efficient access control and user management.</p></p>

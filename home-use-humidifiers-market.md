<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Electronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home use humidifiers come in various types, primarily categorized into ultrasonic and electronic models. Ultrasonic humidifiers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, offering quiet operation and energy efficiency. In contrast, electronic humidifiers use heating elements to boil water, producing steam. While electronic types can effectively eliminate impurities, they may consume more energy and require regular maintenance. Both types aim to enhance indoor air quality, alleviate dryness, and promote overall comfort in living spaces.</p></p>

<p><p>The reclosable bag market is primarily divided into two types: ziplock bags and adhesive self-sealing bags. Ziplock bags feature a press-to-close mechanism that securely seals contents while allowing for easy access. They are widely used for food storage and organization. In contrast, adhesive self-sealing bags have a sticky strip that allows users to close the bag quickly and conveniently without additional tools. These bags are often used for packaging, mailing, and storing smaller items, offering flexibility and protection.</p></p>

<p><p>The helium flow meter market can be categorized into three main types: hydraulic, thermal, and others. Hydraulic flow meters utilize pressure differentials to measure helium flow, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Thermal flow meters gauge flow based on heat transfer principles, offering high accuracy for low flow rates. Other types encompass various technologies like mass flow meters or vortex flow meters, catering to diverse industrial needs and applications, ensuring precise measurements in both research and commercial environments.</p></p>

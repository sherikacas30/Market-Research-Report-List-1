<p><p>The Tool Turrets market is categorized into three main types: Disc-Type, Head-Type, and Crown-Type. Disc-Type turrets utilize a rotating disc mechanism for rapid tool changes, enhancing efficiency in machining operations. Head-Type turrets feature a pivoting head design, allowing for versatile positioning and adjustment of tools. Crown-Type turrets offer a compact design with tools arranged in a circular layout, maximizing space and minimizing setup times. Each type serves distinct applications in manufacturing, catering to varying precision and operational requirements.</p></p>
